ABOUT US

The founder of K.M. Mulla Enterprises Mr. Khaja Moniuddin Mulla is very dynamic and aspiring entrepreneur. Mr. Mulla’s family is very reputed family in Vaddu Villageand always commanded good respect in the village. His family background and good command over hindi language and local language became advantage to Mr. Khaja Moniuddin Mulla to enter into manpower supply business. Being local person he always had and has an advantage of handling labour/worker’s problems and grievances with much ease.

He started his carrier in Mumbai but his first business breakthrough happened in the year 1997 in Tornagallu with contract for supply of man power to Jindal of 12 men. Since then he has no turned back. His man power supply in the year 1998 increased to 52 men. Every year there was a growth of 20 to 30 men. Now he caters around 350 man power requirement, both sem-skilled and unskilled, to JSW in K.M. Mulla Enterprises alone. If one considers the other firms also which are under same group like Sahara Enterprises a proprietary concern of his wife then the overall manpower supply comes to around 450 men.

K.M Mulla Enterprise is VAT, Service Tax, now GST registered concern. The concern also complies the other statutory requirements of provided Fund, ESI, Labour Licence etc., K.M. Mulla group is regular in filing Income-tax Return for past many years. Various work places, currently, K.M Mulla Group is operating are JSW Viyanagar, Jindal Sanjeevini, JSW ARCL , Dolvi, Mumbai, L&T Railway contract, Hubli Lane (Civil Work); JSW Civil Work ( 2 years Back);

Our Mission

The Mulla group intends to cross the 1000 man power supply mark and further diversify into civil work and equipment hiring work. In order to achieve this goal the support of the financial institution is always a great help.
